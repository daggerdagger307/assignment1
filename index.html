A Clothing Company

These days everyone and their sister has a clothing line. What most don’t understand is the dedication and commitment that goes along with running any ongoing creative project. That’s exactly what Dagger Dagger is; a creative project. Blank garments arrive at my shop in sets of 23, to be decorated, and sold in batches of 20. Those 20 shirts will be released for sale once or twice a month. This schedule isn’t a rule, or law, but more of a guideline. All garments are printed in house using industry standard equipment, with originality in mind. One of a kind clothing is the goal. Dagger Dagger for some has been a long time coming, but for most it’s a very new and strange idea. I know everyone is curious to see how long and far this project will go. All I can assure is that I will forever have the need to be creative, and making clothing is the best way I know how to curb those desires.

Process

The majority of our products are screen printed t-shirts, and while the t-shirt will always be a staple to the line, the 5 panel cap is the future of the line. After screen printing for over 10 years, I found myself in need of a new creative challange, and have now curbed that need with production of 5 panel hats. Each hat is individualy cut and sewn here in Canada's Capital. Through trail and error, and the strive for profection, I have created a unique process that alows me to make many hats in a short period of time. On this strong foundation, Dagger Dagger will build it's empire.
